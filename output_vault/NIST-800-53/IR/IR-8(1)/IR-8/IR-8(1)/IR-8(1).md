  \ personally identifiable information:\n  (a) A process to determine if notice to\
  \ individuals or other organizations, including oversight organizations, is needed;\n\
  \  (b) An assessment process to determine the extent of the harm, embarrassment,\
  \ inconvenience, or unfairness to affected individuals and any mechanisms to mitigate\
  \ such harms; and\n  (c) Identification of applicable privacy requirements."
discussion: Organizations may be required by law, regulation, or policy to follow
  specific procedures relating to breaches, including notice to individuals, affected
  organizations, and oversight bodies; standards of harm; and mitigation or other
  specific requirements.
