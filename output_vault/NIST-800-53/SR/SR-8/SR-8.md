---
name: Notification Agreements
text: 'Establish agreements and procedures with entities involved in the supply chain
  for the system, system component, or system service for the [Selection (one or more):
  notification of supply chain compromises; results of assessments or audits; [Assignment:
  organization-defined information]].'
discussion: The establishment of agreements and procedures facilitates communications
  among supply chain entities. Early notification of compromises and potential compromises
  in the supply chain that can potentially adversely affect or have adversely affected
  organizational systems or system components is essential for organizations to effectively
  respond to such incidents. The results of assessments or audits may include open-source
  information that contributed to a decision or result and could be used to help the
  supply chain entity resolve a concern or improve its processes.
---
