---
name: Tamper Resistance and Detection
text: Implement a tamper protection program for the system, system component, or system
  service.
discussion: Anti-tamper technologies, tools, and techniques provide a level of protection
  for systems, system components, and services against many threats, including reverse
  engineering, modification, and substitution. Strong identification combined with
  tamper resistance and/or tamper detection is essential to protecting systems and
  components during distribution and when in use.
