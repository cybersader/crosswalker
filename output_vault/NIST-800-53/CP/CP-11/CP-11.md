---
name: Alternate Communications Protocols
text: 'Provide the capability to employ [Assignment: organization-defined alternative
  communications protocols] in support of maintaining continuity of operations.'
discussion: Contingency plans and the contingency training or testing associated with
  those plans incorporate an alternate communications protocol capability as part
  of establishing resilience in organizational systems. Switching communications protocols
  may affect software applications and operational aspects of systems. Organizations
  assess the potential side effects of introducing alternate communications protocols
  prior to implementation.
