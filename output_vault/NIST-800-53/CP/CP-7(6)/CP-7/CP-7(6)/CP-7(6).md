---
name: Alternate Processing Site | Inability to Return to Primary Site
text: Plan and prepare for circumstances that preclude returning to the primary processing
  site.
discussion: There may be situations that preclude an organization from returning to
  the primary processing site such as if a natural disaster (e.g., flood or a hurricane)
  damaged or destroyed a facility and it was determined that rebuilding in the same
  location was not prudent.
