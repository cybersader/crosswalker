---
name: Security and Privacy Attributes | Attribute Value Changes by Authorized Individuals
text: Provide authorized individuals (or processes acting on behalf of individuals)
  the capability to define or change the value of associated security and privacy
  attributes.
discussion: The content or assigned values of attributes can directly affect the ability
  of individuals to access organizational information. Therefore, it is important
  for systems to be able to limit the ability to create or modify attributes to authorized
  individuals.
---
