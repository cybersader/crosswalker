  reflect changes in organizational mission and business functions, environments of
  operation, technologies, or threats. A periodic review of assigned user privileges
  is necessary to determine if the rationale for assigning such privileges remains
  valid. If the need cannot be revalidated, organizations take appropriate corrective
  actions.
