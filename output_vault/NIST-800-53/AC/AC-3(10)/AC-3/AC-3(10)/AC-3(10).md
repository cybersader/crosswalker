text: 'Employ an audited override of automated access control mechanisms under [Assignment:
  organization-defined conditions] by [Assignment: organization-defined roles].'
discussion: In certain situations, such as when there is a threat to human life or
  an event that threatens the organization's ability to carry out critical missions
  or business functions, an override capability for access control mechanisms may
  be needed. Override conditions are defined by organizations and used only in those
  limited circumstances. Audit events are defined in AU-2. Audit records are generated
  in AU-12.
