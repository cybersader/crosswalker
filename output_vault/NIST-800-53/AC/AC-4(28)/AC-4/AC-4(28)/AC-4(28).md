---
name: Information Flow Enforcement | Linear Filter Pipelines
text: When transferring information between different security domains, implement
  a linear content filter pipeline that is enforced with discretionary and mandatory
  access controls.
discussion: Content filtering is the process of inspecting information as it traverses
  a cross-domain solution and determines if the information meets a predefined policy.
  The use of linear content filter pipelines ensures that filter processes are non-bypassable
  and always invoked. In general, the use of parallel filtering architectures for
  content filtering of a single data type introduces bypass and non-invocation issues.
