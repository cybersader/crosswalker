---
name: Session Termination | Termination Message
text: Display an explicit logout message to users indicating the termination of authenticated
  communications sessions.
discussion: Logout messages for web access can be displayed after authenticated sessions
  have been terminated. However, for certain types of sessions, including file transfer
  protocol (FTP) sessions, systems typically send logout messages as final messages
  prior to terminating sessions.
