  types of data can enhance protection by ensuring that information is not commingled
  while in transit and by enabling flow control by transmission paths that are not
  otherwise achievable. Types of separable information include inbound and outbound
  communications traffic, service requests and responses, and information of differing
  security impact or classification levels.
