name: Previous Logon Notification | Unsuccessful Logons
text: Notify the user, upon successful logon, of the number of unsuccessful logon
  attempts since the last successful logon.
discussion: Information about the number of unsuccessful logon attempts since the
  last successful logon allows the user to recognize if the number of unsuccessful
  logon attempts is consistent with the user's actual logon attempts.
