name: System Monitoring | Privileged Users
text: 'Implement the following additional monitoring of privileged users: [Assignment:
  organization-defined additional monitoring].'
discussion: Privileged users have access to more sensitive information, including
  security-related information, than the general user population. Access to such information
  means that privileged users can potentially do greater damage to systems and organizations
  than non-privileged users. Therefore, implementing additional monitoring on privileged
  users helps to ensure that organizations can identify malicious activity at the
  earliest possible time and take appropriate actions.
