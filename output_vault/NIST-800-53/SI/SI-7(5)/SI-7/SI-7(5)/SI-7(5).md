name: Software, Firmware, and Information Integrity | Automated Response to Integrity
  Violations
text: 'Automatically [Selection (one or more): shut the system down; restart the system;
  implement [Assignment: organization-defined controls]] when integrity violations
  are discovered.'
discussion: Organizations may define different integrity-checking responses by type
  of information, specific information, or a combination of both. Types of information
  include firmware, software, and user data. Specific information includes boot firmware
  for certain types of machines. The automatic implementation of controls within organizational
  systems includes reversing the changes, halting the system, or triggering audit
  alerts when unauthorized modifications to critical security files occur.
