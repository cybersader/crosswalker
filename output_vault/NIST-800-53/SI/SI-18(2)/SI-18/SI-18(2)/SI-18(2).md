---
name: Personally Identifiable Information Quality Operations | Data Tags
text: Employ data tags to automate the correction or deletion of personally identifiable
  information across the information life cycle within organizational systems.
discussion: Data tagging personally identifiable information includes tags that note
  processing permissions, authority to process, de-identification, impact level, information
  life cycle stage, and retention or last updated dates. Employing data tags for personally
  identifiable information can support the use of automation tools to correct or delete
  relevant personally identifiable information.
