---
name: Predictable Failure Prevention | Manual Transfer Between Components
text: 'Manually initiate transfers between active and standby system components when
  the use of the active component reaches [Assignment: organization-defined percentage]
  of the mean time to failure.'
discussion: For example, if the MTTF for a system component is 100 days and the MTTF
  percentage defined by the organization is 90 percent, the manual transfer would
  occur after 90 days.
