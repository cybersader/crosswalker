---
name: Flaw Remediation | Removal of Previous Versions of Software and Firmware
text: 'Remove previous versions of [Assignment: organization-defined software and
  firmware components] after updated versions have been installed.'
discussion: Previous versions of software or firmware components that are not removed
  from the system after updates have been installed may be exploited by adversaries.
  Some products may automatically remove previous versions of software and firmware
  from the system.
