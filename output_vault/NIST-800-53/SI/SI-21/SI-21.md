---
name: Information Refresh
text: 'Refresh [Assignment: organization-defined information] at [Assignment: organization-defined
  frequencies] or generate the information on demand and delete the information when
  no longer needed.'
discussion: Retaining information for longer than it is needed makes it an increasingly
  valuable and enticing target for adversaries. Keeping information available for
  the minimum period of time needed to support organizational missions or business
  functions reduces the opportunity for adversaries to compromise, capture, and exfiltrate
  that information.
