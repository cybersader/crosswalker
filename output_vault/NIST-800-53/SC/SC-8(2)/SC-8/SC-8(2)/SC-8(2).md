text: 'Maintain the [Selection (one or more): confidentiality; integrity] of information
  during preparation for transmission and during reception.'
discussion: Information can be unintentionally or maliciously disclosed or modified
  during preparation for transmission or during reception, including during aggregation,
  at protocol transformation points, and during packing and unpacking. Such unauthorized
  disclosures or modifications compromise the confidentiality or integrity of the
  information.
