name: Wireless Link Protection
text: 'Protect external and internal [Assignment: organization-defined wireless links]
  from the following signal parameter attacks: [Assignment: organization-defined types
  of signal parameter attacks or references to sources for such attacks].'
discussion: Wireless link protection applies to internal and external wireless communication
  links that may be visible to individuals who are not authorized system users. Adversaries
  can exploit the signal parameters of wireless links if such links are not adequately
  protected. There are many ways to exploit the signal parameters of wireless links
  to gain intelligence, deny service, or spoof system users. Protection of wireless
  links reduces the impact of attacks that are unique to wireless systems. If organizations
  rely on commercial service providers for transmission services as commodity items
  rather than as fully dedicated services, it may not be possible to implement wireless
  link protections to the extent necessary to meet organizational security requirements.
