name: Cross Domain Policy Enforcement
text: 'Implement a policy enforcement mechanism [Selection: physically; logically]
  between the physical and/or network interfaces for the connecting security domains.'
discussion: For logical policy enforcement mechanisms, organizations avoid creating
  a logical path between interfaces to prevent the ability to bypass the policy enforcement
  mechanism. For physical policy enforcement mechanisms, the robustness of physical
  isolation afforded by the physical implementation of policy enforcement to preclude
  the presence of logical covert channels penetrating the security domain may be needed.
  Contact ncdsmo@nsa.gov for more information.
