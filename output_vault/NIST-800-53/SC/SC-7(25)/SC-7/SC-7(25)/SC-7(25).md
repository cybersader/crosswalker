name: Boundary Protection | Unclassified National Security System Connections
text: 'Prohibit the direct connection of [Assignment: organization-defined unclassified
  national security system] to an external network without the use of [Assignment:
  organization-defined boundary protection device].'
discussion: A direct connection is a dedicated physical or virtual connection between
  two or more systems. Organizations typically do not have complete control over external
  networks, including the Internet. Boundary protection devices (e.g., firewalls,
  gateways, and routers) mediate communications and information flows between unclassified
  national security systems and external networks.
