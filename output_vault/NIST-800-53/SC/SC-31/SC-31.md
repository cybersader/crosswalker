name: Covert Channel Analysis
text: "|-\n  a. Perform a covert channel analysis to identify those aspects of communications\
  \ within the system that are potential avenues for covert [Selection (one or more):\
  \ storage; timing] channels; and\n  b. Estimate the maximum bandwidth of those channels."
discussion: Developers are in the best position to identify potential areas within
  systems that might lead to covert channels. Covert channel analysis is a meaningful
  activity when there is the potential for unauthorized information flows across security
  domains, such as in the case of systems that contain export-controlled information
  and have connections to external networks (i.e., networks that are not controlled
  by organizations). Covert channel analysis is also useful for multilevel secure
  systems, multiple security level systems, and cross-domain systems.
