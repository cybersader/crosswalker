text: 'Implement cryptographic mechanisms to reduce the detection potential of wireless
  links to [Assignment: organization-defined level of reduction].'
discussion: The implementation of cryptographic mechanisms to reduce detection potential
  is used for covert communications and to protect wireless transmitters from geo-location.
  It also ensures that the spread spectrum waveforms used to achieve a low probability
  of detection are not predictable by unauthorized individuals. Mission requirements,
  projected threats, concept of operations, and applicable laws, executive orders,
  directives, regulations, policies, and standards determine the levels to which wireless
  links are undetectable.
