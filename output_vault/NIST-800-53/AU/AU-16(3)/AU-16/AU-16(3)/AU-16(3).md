---
name: Cross-organizational Audit Logging | Disassociability
text: 'Implement [Assignment: organization-defined measures] to disassociate individuals
  from audit information transmitted across organizational boundaries.'
discussion: Preserving identities in audit trails could have privacy ramifications,
  such as enabling the tracking and profiling of individuals, but may not be operationally
  necessary. These risks could be further amplified when transmitting information
  across organizational boundaries. Implementing privacy-enhancing cryptographic techniques
  can disassociate individuals from audit information and reduce privacy risk while
  maintaining accountability.
