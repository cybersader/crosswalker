---
name: Response to Audit Logging Process Failures | Configurable Traffic Volume Thresholds
text: 'Enforce configurable network communications traffic volume thresholds reflecting
  limits on audit log storage capacity and [Selection: reject; delay] network traffic
  above those thresholds.'
discussion: Organizations have the capability to reject or delay the processing of
  network communications traffic if audit logging information about such traffic is
  determined to exceed the storage capacity of the system audit logging function.
  The rejection or delay response is triggered by the established organizational traffic
  volume thresholds that can be adjusted based on changes to audit log storage capacity.
