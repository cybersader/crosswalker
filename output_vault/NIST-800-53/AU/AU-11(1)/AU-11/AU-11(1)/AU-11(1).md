---
name: Audit Record Retention | Long-term Retrieval Capability
text: 'Employ [Assignment: organization-defined measures] to ensure that long-term
  audit records generated by the system can be retrieved.'
discussion: Organizations need to access and read audit records requiring long-term
  storage (on the order of years). Measures employed to help facilitate the retrieval
  of audit records include converting records to newer formats, retaining equipment
  capable of reading the records, and retaining the necessary documentation to help
  personnel understand how to interpret the records.
