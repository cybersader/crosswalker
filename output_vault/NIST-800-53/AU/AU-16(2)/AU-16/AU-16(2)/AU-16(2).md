discussion: Due to the distributed nature of the audit information, cross-organization
  sharing of audit information may be essential for effective analysis of the auditing
  being performed. For example, the audit records of one organization may not provide
  sufficient information to determine the appropriate or inappropriate use of organizational
  information resources by individuals in other organizations. In some instances,
  only individuals' home organizations have the appropriate knowledge to make such
  determinations, thus requiring the sharing of audit information among organizations.
