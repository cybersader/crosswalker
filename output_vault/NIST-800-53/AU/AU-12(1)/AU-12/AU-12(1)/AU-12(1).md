  into a system-wide (logical or physical) audit trail that is time-correlated to
  within [Assignment: organization-defined level of tolerance for the relationship
  between time stamps of individual records in the audit trail].'

  audit records can be reliably related to the time stamps in other audit records
  to achieve a time ordering of the records within organizational tolerances.
