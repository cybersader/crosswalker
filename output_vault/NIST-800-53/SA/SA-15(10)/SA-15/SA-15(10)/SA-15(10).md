---
name: Development Process, Standards, and Tools | Incident Response Plan
text: Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to
  provide, implement, and test an incident response plan.
discussion: The incident response plan provided by developers may provide information
  not readily available to organizations and be incorporated into organizational incident
  response plans. Developer information may also be extremely helpful, such as when
  organizations respond to vulnerabilities in commercial off-the-shelf products.
