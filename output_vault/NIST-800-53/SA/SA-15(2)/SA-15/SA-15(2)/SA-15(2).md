---
name: Development Process, Standards, and Tools | Security and Privacy Tracking Tools
text: Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to
  select and employ security and privacy tracking tools for use during the development
  process.
discussion: System development teams select and deploy security and privacy tracking
  tools, including vulnerability or work item tracking systems that facilitate assignment,
  sorting, filtering, and tracking of completed work items or tasks associated with
  development processes.
