---
name: Development Process, Standards, and Tools | Reuse of Threat and Vulnerability
  Information
text: Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to
  use threat modeling and vulnerability analyses from similar systems, components,
  or services to inform the current development process.
discussion: Analysis of vulnerabilities found in similar software applications can
  inform potential design and implementation issues for systems under development.
  Similar systems or system components may exist within developer organizations. Vulnerability
  information is available from a variety of public and private sector sources, including
  the NIST National Vulnerability Database.
