---
name: Acquisition Process | Functional Properties of Controls
text: Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to
  provide a description of the functional properties of the controls to be implemented.
discussion: Functional properties of security and privacy controls describe the functionality
  (i.e., security or privacy capability, functions, or mechanisms) visible at the
  interfaces of the controls and specifically exclude functionality and data structures
  internal to the operation of the controls.
---
