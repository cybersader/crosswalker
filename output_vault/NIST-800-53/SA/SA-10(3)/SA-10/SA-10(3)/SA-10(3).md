---
name: Developer Configuration Management | Hardware Integrity Verification
text: Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to
  enable integrity verification of hardware components.
discussion: Hardware integrity verification allows organizations to detect unauthorized
  changes to hardware components using developer-provided tools, techniques, methods,
  and mechanisms. Organizations may verify the integrity of hardware components with
  hard-to-copy labels, verifiable serial numbers provided by developers, and by requiring
  the use of anti-tamper technologies. Delivered hardware components also include
  hardware and firmware updates to such components.
