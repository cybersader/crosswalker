---
name: Developer Configuration Management | Software and Firmware Integrity Verification
text: Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to
  enable integrity verification of software and firmware components.
discussion: Software and firmware integrity verification allows organizations to detect
  unauthorized changes to software and firmware components using developer-provided
  tools, techniques, and mechanisms. The integrity checking mechanisms can also address
  counterfeiting of software and firmware components. Organizations verify the integrity
  of software and firmware components, for example, through secure one-way hashes
  provided by developers. Delivered software and firmware components also include
  any updates to such components.
