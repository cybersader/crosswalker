---
name: System Development Life Cycle | Manage Preproduction Environment
text: Protect system preproduction environments commensurate with risk throughout
  the system development life cycle for the system, system component, or system service.
discussion: The preproduction environment includes development, test, and integration
  environments. The program protection planning processes established by the Department
  of Defense are examples of managing the preproduction environment for defense contractors.
  Criticality analysis and the application of controls on developers also contribute
  to a more secure system development environment.
