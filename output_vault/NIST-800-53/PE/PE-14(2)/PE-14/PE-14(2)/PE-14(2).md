  of changes potentially harmful to personnel or equipment to [Assignment: organization-defined
  personnel or roles].'
discussion: The alarm or notification may be an audible alarm or a visual message
  in real time to personnel or roles defined by the organization. Such alarms and
  notifications can help minimize harm to individuals and damage to organizational
  assets by facilitating a timely incident response.
