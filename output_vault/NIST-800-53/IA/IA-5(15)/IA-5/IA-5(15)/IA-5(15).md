---
name: Authenticator Management | GSA-approved Products and Services
text: Use only General Services Administration-approved products and services for
  identity, credential, and access management.
discussion: General Services Administration (GSA)-approved products and services are
  products and services that have been approved through the GSA conformance program,
  where applicable, and posted to the GSA Approved Products List. GSA provides guidance
  for teams to design and build functional and secure systems that comply with Federal
  Identity, Credential, and Access Management (FICAM) policies, technologies, and
  implementation patterns.
