---
name: Authenticator Management | Presentation Attack Detection for Biometric Authenticators
text: Employ presentation attack detection mechanisms for biometric-based authentication.
discussion: Biometric characteristics do not constitute secrets. Such characteristics
  can be obtained by online web accesses, taking a picture of someone with a camera
  phone to obtain facial images with or without their knowledge, lifting from objects
  that someone has touched (e.g., a latent fingerprint), or capturing a high-resolution
  image (e.g., an iris pattern). Presentation attack detection technologies including
  liveness detection, can mitigate the risk of these types of attacks by making it
  difficult to produce artifacts intended to defeat the biometric sensor.
