---
name: Consent | Tailored Consent
text: 'Provide [Assignment: organization-defined mechanisms] to allow individuals
  to tailor processing permissions to selected elements of personally identifiable
  information.'
discussion: While some processing may be necessary for the basic functionality of
  the product or service, other processing may not. In these circumstances, organizations
  allow individuals to select how specific personally identifiable information elements
  may be processed. More tailored consent may help reduce privacy risk, increase individual
  satisfaction, and avoid adverse behaviors, such as abandonment of the product or
  service.
