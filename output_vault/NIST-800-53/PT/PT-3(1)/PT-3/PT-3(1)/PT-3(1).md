  processing purposes].'
discussion: Data tags support the tracking of processing purposes by conveying the
  purposes along with the relevant elements of personally identifiable information
  throughout the system. By conveying the processing purposes in a data tag along
  with the personally identifiable information as the information transits a system,
  a system owner or operator can identify whether a change in processing would be
  compatible with the identified and documented purposes. Data tags may also support
  the use of automated tools.
