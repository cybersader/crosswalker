---
name: Specific Categories of Personally Identifiable Information | First Amendment
  Information
text: Prohibit the processing of information describing how any individual exercises
  rights guaranteed by the First Amendment unless expressly authorized by statute
  or by the individual or unless pertinent to and within the scope of an authorized
  law enforcement activity.
discussion: The PRIVACT limits agencies' ability to process information that describes
  how individuals exercise rights guaranteed by the First Amendment. Organizations
  consult with the senior agency official for privacy and legal counsel regarding
  these requirements.
