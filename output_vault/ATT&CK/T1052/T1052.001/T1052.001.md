---
title: 'Exfiltration Over Physical Medium: Exfiltration over USB'
desc: Adversaries may attempt to exfiltrate data over a USB connected physical device.
  In certain circumstances, such as an air-gapped network compromise, exfiltration
  could occur via a USB device introduced by a user. The USB device could be used
  as the final exfiltration point or to hop between otherwise disconnected systems.
