  \ be modified by an adversary to perform or assist in malicious activity. Capabilities\
  \ exist to overwrite the system firmware, which may give sophisticated adversaries\
  \ a means to install malicious firmware updates as a means of persistence on a system\
  \ that may be difficult to detect."
