  \ Symantec WastedLocker June 2020) With a sufficient level of access, creating such\
  \ accounts may be used to establish secondary credentialed access that do not require\
  \ persistent remote access tools to be deployed on the system.\n  \n  Accounts may\
  \ be created on the local system or within a domain or cloud tenant. In cloud environments,\
  \ adversaries may create accounts that only have access to specific services, which\
  \ can reduce the chance of detection."
