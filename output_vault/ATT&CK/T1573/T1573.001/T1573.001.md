---
title: 'Encrypted Channel: Symmetric Cryptography'
desc: Adversaries may employ a known symmetric encryption algorithm to conceal command
  and control traffic rather than relying on any inherent protections provided by
  a communication protocol. Symmetric encryption algorithms use the same key for plaintext
  encryption and ciphertext decryption. Common symmetric encryption algorithms include
  AES, DES, 3DES, Blowfish, and RC4.
