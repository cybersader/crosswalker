---
title: Inventory and Control of Enterprise Assets
description: Actively manage (inventory, track, and correct) all enterprise assets
  (end-user devices, including portable and mobile; network devices; non-computing/Internet
  of Things (IoT) devices; and servers) connected to the infrastructure physically,
  virtually, remotely, and those within cloud environments, to accurately know the
  totality of assets that need to be monitored and protected within the enterprise.
  This will also support identifying unauthorized and unmanaged assets to remove or
  remediate.
